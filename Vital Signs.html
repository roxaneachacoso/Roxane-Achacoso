Vital Signs Reference Guide

A quick overview of normal ranges by age

Normal values for each of these vital signs vary by age and, in some cases, by sex. They may also vary based on weight, exercise capability, and overall health.

Normal Vital Signs for Healthy Adults at Rest

  • Heart rate (pulse): 60–100 bpm
  • Respiratory rate: 12–18 breaths per minute
  • Blood pressure: 90/60 mmHg to 120/80 mmHg
  • Temperature: 97.8°F (36.5°C) to 99.1°F (37.3°C); average 98.6°F (37°C)
